OUR MISSION

We bring joy to millions
of people by creating
high quality games

We are one of the leading
mobile game developers in the world


Coolgc is a mobile game development company founded in 2004. We started off as
a casual game developer for PC and created several award-winning titles back then.
Since 2011, we have been focusing on free-to-play games for smartphones and tablets.
Our games, Homescapes, Gardenscapes, Township, Fishdom have consistently been
among the Top 50 Grossing Apps for iOS and Google Play since their release.

Our team


We are a distributed team of over 1000

quality is what matters most to us - with that in

Our culture


At Coolgc, we have a very simple vision for achieving great results - we think
the output should come on top of the hours you put in. This vision has
pretty much shaped our culture and our approach to the work. We are
always hungry for knowledge and ready to take up new challenges as they
are the driving force that helps us make our games better. We also believe
that a sense of personal responsibility and commitment to the mission are
what really make us get up in the morning and get things done.
After all, we love what we do!
